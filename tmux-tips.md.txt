## These are the commands with the default prefix "CTRL+b"

Split panes vertically:
CTRL+b %

Split panes horizontally:
CTRL+b "

Show pane numbers:
CTRL+b q

Change panes:
CTRL+b <arrow-key>

Close pane:
CTRL+d

Toggle current pane into full screen:
CTRL+b z

Add new window:
CTRL+b c

List open windows:
CTRL+b w

Switch between windows:
CTRL+b <window-number>

Detach from session (e.g. close tmux):
CTRL+b d

List available commands:
CTRL+b ?

Show session:
tmux ls

Show current session:
CTRL+b s
